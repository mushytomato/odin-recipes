Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a delightful Italian dish that boasts layers of rich flavors and textures. To prepare this classic comfort food, you'll need the following ingredients: lasagna noodles, lean ground beef or Italian sausage, crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced onions, minced garlic, dried basil, oregano, salt, black pepper, ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and fresh parsley.

Begin by preheating your oven to the recommended temperature. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage until browned, then drain the excess fat. Stir in the diced onions and minced garlic, sautéing until they become fragrant. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste, and season the sauce with dried basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Allow the mixture to simmer on low heat for about 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

While the sauce simmers, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with some chopped fresh parsley and a pinch of salt.

Now it's time to assemble the lasagna. In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top, followed by dollops of the ricotta cheese mixture and a generous sprinkle of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan. Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of noodles on top. Spread any remaining meat sauce and cheeses over the last layer of noodles.

Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese on top turns golden and bubbly.

Once it's done, take the lasagna out of the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before cutting and serving. The result is a mouthwatering masterpiece of savory flavors, creamy cheese, and tender noodles, sure to delight family and friends with every delicious bite. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (oven-ready or traditional, depending on your preference)
  • Lean ground beef or Italian sausage (about 1 pound or 500 grams)
  • Crushed tomatoes (canned, about 28 ounces or 800 grams)
  • Tomato sauce (canned, about 14 ounces or 400 grams)
  • Tomato paste (small can, about 6 ounces or 170 grams)
  • Diced onions (1 medium-sized onion)
  • Minced garlic (3-4 cloves)
  • Dried basil (1 teaspoon)
  • Ricotta cheese (about 15 ounces or 425 grams)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese (about 2 cups or 200 grams)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (about 1 cup or 100 grams)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the recommended temperature.
  2. Brown lean ground beef or Italian sausage in a skillet, then drain excess fat.
  3. Add diced onions and minced garlic to the skillet, sauté until fragrant.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Season with dried basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Let it simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with chopped fresh parsley and a pinch of salt.
  7. Layer the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, then add a layer of noodles, ricotta mixture, shredded mozzarella, and grated Parmesan. Repeat the layers.
  8. Finish with a final layer of noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses on top.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before cutting and serving.
